      Printed logo Placement Sides:

      • 1. You can't have letters that are less than 0.25 inches or they won't embroider well (Smaller letter size will decrease the detail of the embroidery).
      • 2. Gradients - Stitches do not blend so the gradient will be less smooth.
      • 3. Preferred Format - Vector files (cdr, ai, eps, pdf) for highest quality embroidery.
      • 4. Acceptable Format - Raster files (jpg, tif, gif, png, eps) at a minimum of 150dpi at the final image size.
      • 5. Pixel graphics are composed of pixels rather than paths so embroidery may not be as exact as you would like it to be.
